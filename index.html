The Tranquility of LEGO.

®

Courtesy of: Design Rush

Lego is my sanctuary. It brings me such a sense of peace to take the time to build a new set. Building lego sets can take me anywhere from a couple hours to a full week for completion. Creating new builds has taken over my hobby time and it is so rewarding.

LEGO sets I've built

Vespa I & Vespa Mini

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

Now on display in my entertainment room are Vespa I and Vespa Mini. This set took under 3 hours to complete. I find it helpful to put on a new movie to help the time fly. Having a media distraction on in the background also minimizes my stress if something goes wrong while building.

Tangled

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

The Rapunzel set took me the shortest time to build. This took about 15 mins to complete. I have always loved the movie Tangled since I was little. It was only right that I had a Tangled set somewhere in my home! This set currently resides in the entryway now in all her glory.

Easter basket

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

I love putting this Easter Basket set out on display during the holiday time. I got this set from Facebook Marketplace sometime last year. It took me about 30 mins to complete with all the tiny pieces. Sadly there is no link available to this set as of now.

Double Christmas

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

Currently residing in my room are my two Christmas Trees. This set came in one box as a two for one build. Naturally I had to build it into to two sets. During the holiday time, I set each one in different rooms to have extra christmas spirit around the house! Lego thinks of everything when it comes to sets you can have on display.

Rose Bouquet

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

For Valetine's Day this year, I was gifted these beautiful Roses. I love roses, but hate to watch them fade. This was the perfect set for me! Aside from a light dusting once awhile, these roses are going nowhere! Now I rearrange the flowers in different vases I have around the house.

WildFlowers Bundle

Courtesy of: Matthew's iPhone

I built the Wildflowers a few months ago! Flower lego sets dont take me too long to complete. However, this full bundle took me two hours. This full arrangement is now mixed with other floral builds I've done. There are about five colorful lego floral builds around the house.
